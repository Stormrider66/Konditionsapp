# Skelleftea Pilot Data Privacy Baseline

This is the minimum privacy baseline for a development pilot with elite and junior athletes.

## Data Ownership

- The club owns club-entered roster, test, training, and staff workflow data.
- Athletes own their personal profile data and should be able to request export or deletion.
- Trainomics may use anonymized product learnings from the pilot, but no identifiable athlete data in sales material without written approval.

## Access Roles

- OWNER/ADMIN: full club overview and staff management.
- COACH: coaching, testing, programs, AI workflows.
- PHYSICAL_TRAINER: team-scoped performance workflows for assigned teams.
- ASSISTANT_COACH: team-scoped testing/interval support.
- PHYSIO: injury, rehab, restrictions, and medical workflow access for assigned teams.

## Junior Athletes

- Get guardian/club-approved consent before enabling athlete login for minors.
- Start juniors in staff-only mode when consent status is unclear.
- Avoid entering sensitive health notes unless they are needed for training safety.
- Keep acute injury and medical notes in physio workflows, not free-text roster notes.

## Pilot Consent Text

Use this short text in the club kickoff:

> Trainomics is used by Skelleftea AIK staff to collect and review training, testing, readiness, and performance data during a development pilot. Access is limited to assigned staff roles. Athlete data is not used publicly or in sales material unless it is anonymized or separately approved.

## Go-Live Gate

- Staff list and team assignments reviewed.
- Athlete portal invitations only sent to approved athletes.
- Junior consent path agreed with the club.
- Reply-to/contact inbox configured for privacy questions.
- Export/delete request owner named internally.

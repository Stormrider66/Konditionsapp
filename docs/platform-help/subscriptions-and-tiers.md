# Subscriptions and Tiers

What each athlete plan (Free, Standard, Pro, Elite) and coach plan (Starter, Professional, Business, Enterprise) includes, how trials work, and where to manage billing.

## What it is

The platform has two separate subscription tracks. Athletes subscribe to unlock self-service features (AI chat, integrations, program generation, video analysis). Coaches subscribe to unlock athlete capacity and coaching tools. Both are billed in SEK through Stripe, monthly or yearly.

## Where to find it

- **Athletes:** athlete menu → **Subscription**. Shows your current plan, AI credit balance, top-up packs, and upgrade options.
- **Coaches:** coach menu → **Subscription**. Shows tiers, current athlete count versus your limit, and opens Stripe checkout or the customer portal for changes.

## How it works

### Athlete tiers

| Plan | Price | Monthly AI allowance |
|---|---|---|
| Free | 0 kr | 3 kr |
| Standard | 199 kr/month or 1 990 kr/year | 30 kr |
| Pro | 399 kr/month or 3 990 kr/year | 75 kr |
| Elite | Custom pricing set by your coach's business | 150 kr (business can customize) |

**Free** — view test reports, basic profile, training history, and a small AI chat allowance (capped at 10 chat messages per month). No integrations, no program generation, no workout logging or daily check-in.

**Standard** — everything in Free plus workout logging, daily check-in, Strava/Garmin/Concept2 sync, AI program generation (self-coached), coach requests, nutrition planning, lactate photo import (OCR), and a 50-message monthly chat cap with a bigger AI credit pot.

**Pro** — everything in Standard plus video analysis, advanced intelligence features, self-service program templates, live voice coaching during workouts, a 500-message monthly chat cap, and the larger AI credit pot.

**Elite** — everything in Pro with unlimited chat messages, the largest (customizable) AI allowance, and personal coach/PT follow-up. Elite pricing is set individually by the business that coaches you.

**Athlete trial.** New athletes automatically get a 14-day Standard trial, including a 15 kr trial AI allowance. When the trial ends, features requiring a paid plan lock until you subscribe.

**AI credits and top-ups.** The included allowance resets on the 1st of each month. You can buy extra credits anytime on the Subscription page: AI 50 (49 kr → 50 kr credits), AI 120 (99 kr → 120 kr), AI 275 (199 kr → 275 kr). Top-up credits are used after your included allowance is spent and expire 180 days after purchase.

### Coach tiers

| Plan (display name) | Price | Athlete limit |
|---|---|---|
| Starter (free) | 0 kr | 1 athlete |
| Professional | 499 kr/month or 4 990 kr/year | up to 20 athletes |
| Business | 1 499 kr/month or 14 990 kr/year | up to 100 athletes |
| Enterprise | Custom (contact sales) | unlimited |

**Starter** — try the platform free with 1 athlete, basic test reports, training zones, and email support. No AI generation, program generation, or video analysis.

**Professional** — up to 20 athletes, all test reports, AI program generation (basic), training programs, daily check-in, messaging, email support.

**Business** — up to 100 athletes, everything in Professional plus the full AI Studio, video analysis, white-label reports, advanced analytics, priority support, and limited API access.

**Enterprise** — unlimited athletes, everything in Business plus a dedicated account manager, custom integrations, full API access, and SLA guarantee.

**Coach trial.** New coaches start on a 14-day trial. When it expires, paid features lock until you upgrade.

### Managing your subscription

Upgrades happen on the Subscription pages via Stripe checkout (card payment; yearly billing gives roughly two months free). Existing paying coaches manage payment methods, invoices, and cancellation through the Stripe customer portal opened from the same page. Cancelling keeps access until the end of the paid period.

## Common questions

**Q: What happens if I (coach) have more athletes than my plan allows?**
A: You keep access to all existing athletes, but you cannot add new ones until you upgrade or remove athletes.

**Q: Do I get a trial as a new athlete?**
A: Yes — a 14-day Standard trial starts automatically, including a 15 kr AI allowance. After it ends you drop to Free features until you subscribe.

**Q: When do my AI credits reset, and do unused credits carry over?**
A: The included allowance resets at the start of every calendar month and does not carry over. Purchased top-up credits do carry over and last 180 days.

**Q: Can I switch plans at any time?**
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade whenever you like; upgrades take effect immediately.

**Q: How do I get the Elite athlete plan?**
A: Elite is offered and priced by the business/coach you train with — contact them; it isn't self-service with a fixed price.

**Q: What payment methods work?**
A: Card payments via Stripe. Enterprise coach plans can arrange invoicing via sales contact.
